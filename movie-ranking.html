Top 5 Webseries of 2023...

1. Asur

"Asur" is an Indian crime thriller web series that blends elements of mythology and modern forensic science. Set against the backdrop of Varanasi, it follows a former forensic expert who is drawn back into his old profession by a series of brutal and ritualistic murders, leading to a gripping cat-and-mouse chase with a mysterious serial killer who believes himself to be an incarnation of a demonic figure from ancient lore.

2. Mirzapur

"Mirzapur" is an Indian crime thriller web series that delves into the lawlessness and power struggles in the eponymous town of Mirzapur in Uttar Pradesh. Centered around the ambitions of two brothers, Guddu and Bablu Pandit, and the ruthless mafia boss Kaleen Bhaiya, the series explores themes of violence, revenge, and the quest for dominance in a corrupt and brutal landscape.

Click here to watch Mirzapur

3. The Family Man

"The Family Man" is an Indian espionage thriller web series that follows Srikant Tiwari, a middle-class man secretly working as an intelligence officer for the National Investigation Agency, balancing his high-stakes job and family life. Created by Raj Nidimoru and Krishna D.K., the show blends action, drama, and humor, offering a gripping narrative about national security and personal dilemmas.

Click here to watch The Family Man

4. Panchayat

"Panchayat" is an Indian comedy-drama web series that follows the story of Abhishek, an engineering graduate who reluctantly takes up a job as a secretary in a remote village panchayat, navigating the quirks and challenges of rural life. The series is praised for its humorous yet poignant portrayal of village dynamics and bureaucracy.

5. Paatal Lok

"Paatal Lok" is an Indian crime thriller web series that delves into the dark and gritty underworld of society, following a disillusioned cop's investigation into a high-profile case, revealing the intertwined complexities of crime, politics, and media.
